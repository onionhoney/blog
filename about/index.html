About me

Oct 12, 2017

I'm a third year UCLA undergraduate studying Computer Science, with technical breadth in math.

I'm a passionate competitive programmer who loves to solve technically interesting problems. I am the Committee Chair of UCLA's ACM ICPC club, and I've been organize trainings for a year now, all to build a better learning community.

Meanwhile, I believe present-day coding is the wrong medium of thought. I crave to design beautiful interfaces for humans, to create tools that remove the need for software developers, so they can focus on the bigger picture without the nitty-gritty of coding.

For that ultimate purpose, I'm doing Programming Language research on program verification now, and I also taught myself both frontend and backend techniques. I'm still learning all I can to get further along this road.
